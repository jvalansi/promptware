Common Questions

Do you see our prompts?

Promptware inspects prompts only to classify complexity (a lightweight embedding score — not stored). Your data is never logged, sold, or used to train models. We see metadata (token counts, model routed) not content.

What if a simple request gets routed to the cheap model incorrectly?

The RouteLLM classifier is calibrated conservatively — it degrades quality by less than 5% on MMLU and MT-Bench. You can also set a quality threshold per customer to tune the cutoff toward cost or quality.

Do you hold our OpenAI API key?

No. You pass your own key in the Authorization header on every request, exactly like you do with OpenAI directly. We proxy it but never store it.

What models are supported?

Beta: gpt-4o and gpt-4o-mini routing (OpenAI only). Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini routing are on the roadmap.

Can I self-host RouteLLM instead?

Absolutely — RouteLLM is open-source. Promptware is for teams that don't want to operate the infrastructure, tune thresholds, maintain uptime, and build the logging layer themselves.
